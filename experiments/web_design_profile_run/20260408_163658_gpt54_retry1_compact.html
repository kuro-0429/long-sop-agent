Demo brief

What a first FluxGrid pilot usually includes.

Week 1 — map one high-volume path

Choose a single repeatable queue such as inbound lead routing, ticket classification, payment exception handling, or internal access requests.

Week 2 — add guardrails

Define confidence thresholds, mandatory approvals, business rules, and failure fallbacks. The workspace stays fast because risky paths are explicit.

Week 3 — launch with replay and reporting

Go live with event history, operator notes, throughput tracking, and a replayable runtime. The goal is a calmer queue, not a mysterious black box.
